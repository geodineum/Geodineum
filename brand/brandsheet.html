Where it comes from

The logo is the math.

Not drawn to look geometric — generated from the exact parametric surface the flagship gIris interface renders in 3D. Deterministic input, deterministic mark. That is the whole brand in one object.

φtwisted = φ + (θ · TWIST)
x = (R + r·cos(φtwisted)) · cos θ
y = (R + r·cos(φtwisted)) · sin θ
z =      r·sin(φtwisted)
R = 3.2
major radius — the ring
r = 1.35
tube cross-section
HEX_SIDES = 6
hexagonal tube → the six-fold
TWIST = 1.0
one full turn of the section per revolution
